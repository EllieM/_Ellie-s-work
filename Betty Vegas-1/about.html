About Us

Betty Vegas Salon is your downtown Minneapolis location for professional salon services.

Our experienced stylists offer the latest in hair styles and treatments We also offer a full line of trusted hair care products including shampoos, styling gels and hairsprays. We will help you choose a product that works best with your hair type and style.

Our Staff

Lynn Johnston, Salon Manager

Lynn is a master colorist-stylist with a very creative approach. She has been a hairstylist/manager since the opening of The Hair Police in September 1986. Working there for over 14 years, she specialized in coloring, cutting, dreadlocks, weaves and hair extensions. She most recently worked at the Guthrie Theater, styling and maintaining wigs and hair pieces. With a holistic approach to life, Lynn is a passionate gardener, avid scuba diver and an excellent cook. Besides her passion to organic cooking, house remodeling and recycling, she loves spending time with her two poodles.


Zuhal Sari, Cosmetologist

“I have been interested in hair and fashion all my life. After finishing cosmetology school in 2006 I started to work in the hair salon environment. When I cut hair I enjoy the creativity and being able to meet new people all the time. It relaxes me and puts a big smile on my face. I love what I do.”
